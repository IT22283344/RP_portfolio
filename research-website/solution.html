Our Solution

Comprehensive AI Tools for Coastal Fishing

By combining environmental intelligence, market analysis, and deep learning, our platform provides actionable solutions for sustainable and efficient operations.

Ocean Data Market Predictor Quality Grader Trip Planner

Fish Zone Prediction with Ocean Data Intelligence

We provide an AI-based system that predicts fish-rich zones using environmental and oceanographic data such as sea surface temperature, chlorophyll concentration, and ocean currents. The system analyzes these factors using machine learning models to identify high-probability fishing areas.

This enables fishermen to reduce search time, save fuel, and increase catch efficiency by targeting the most suitable fishing locations based on real-time and historical data patterns.

Fish Price Prediction with Market Analysis

Our platform includes a machine learning-based price prediction system that analyzes historical price trends, seasonal patterns, and external factors such as weather and demand.

The system generates future price estimates to help fishermen make better selling decisions, improving profitability and reducing the risk of price fluctuations in the market.

AI-Based Fish Quality Grading System

We implement a deep learning-based image classification system to automatically assess fish quality. Using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), the system analyzes fish images to classify freshness and grade levels.

This ensures consistent and accurate quality evaluation, reducing manual errors and supporting better pricing and handling decisions in the fisheries supply chain.

Smart Trip Cost Prediction and Planning

The system estimates trip cost and fuel consumption using a hybrid approach combining mathematical models and machine learning. Factors such as travel distance, engine power, weather conditions, and trip duration are analyzed to generate cost predictions.

This helps fishermen plan trips more efficiently, reduce unnecessary expenses, and improve overall operational decision-making.
